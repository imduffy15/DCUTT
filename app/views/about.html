DCU Timetables

What is DCUTT

DCUTT supplies a nicely formatted version of Dublin City University programme timetables that are optimized for viewing on a mobile screen.

If you are an Android user you can download our application on the Google Play

If you are a iOS user check out this youtube video to see how to add DCUTT to your home screen

How does it work?

On a weekly bases our system pulls the iCal versions of the timetables from Dublin City University servers. It parses them and outputs them in a format that your device can quickly extract information from

Who made it?
